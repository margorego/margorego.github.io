about me

My name is Margo Rego and I'm a designer-to-be from Southcoast Massachusetts. I am currently a sophomore studing Graphic Design at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth. I find my inspiration in colors, patterns, and the outdoors. My goal in life is to work for a meaningful company to make modern graphics and designs for them.

Other than graphic design, my life revolves around the outdoors. I am very passionate about rock climbing, hiking, and sustainability. I have been climbing since I was 15, and have never looked back. The amazingly supportive and passionate people I have met through climbing help to inspire me to be a better designer and create work I am passionate about. Climbing has taken me throughout the United States and I am so greatful to say I am truly living my best life.
